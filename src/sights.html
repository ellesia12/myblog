Cool Sights to See in Lisbon

Check out our list of top sights to see in Lisbon!

Praca do Commercio

Praca do Commercio, in English: Commerce Square, is located in the heart of Lisbon,situated near the Tagus river. Before the great earthquake of 1755, this square was the courtyard of the royal palace. There are many beautiful restaurants here and it is a great place to snap a picture!

Discovering Churches

Portugal has a long history of Catholism, dating back to the Roman Empire. 84% of the Portguese population is baptised Catholic. There are tons of amazing churches in Portgual. Don't miss out on this culture experience of a lifetime.
